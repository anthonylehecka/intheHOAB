It is of little wonder that the shared history of the people of Earth is filled with stories and sayings of the heart. For it is within our hearts, and by that is meant the innermost organ of our being, where the direct light of God first enters into each of us, that we take that light and either let it shine out unimpeded, without alteration, gloriously brilliant for the benefit of everyone around us, or else we force it to bend, twist and darken on a journey through the impurities we hold within our hearts.
A heart that is pure is incapable of performing evil deeds. For as the source of our being is Absolute Goodness, so then are the thoughts, words and deeds of a heart that has been purified and is as taintless as its source.
Blessed are the pure in heart: for they shall see God
